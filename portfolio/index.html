About

The person behind the pixels.

I started this site when learning to code back in 2018, with the domain name Tookay.net being a reference to the ever-present background music of Too-kay (the Southeast Asian word for geckos) in Cambodia, where I lived/worked at the time. Also a bit of a play on the year 2000, which is when I first travelled extensively in the region. I'm a developer and university administrator/manager who enjoys building useful tools and applications for the web, from score trackers to full-stack apps. Now playing extensively with AI development tools.
