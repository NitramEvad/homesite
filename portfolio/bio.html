About Me

Manager. Builder.

Who I Am

I branched out into software engineering after 14 years in university admin and management (SOAS and University of Cambridge), re-training into the Javascript ecosystem via a bootcamp in 2021. This was followed by three, somewhat grim, years as a junior dev in two, now-defunct, startups.

Now back in the world of university management, I primarily utilise low-code (MS Power Apps and Power Automate flows) to streamline and automate office and administrative practices, ideally in way that is sustainable and easy to manage for non-tech proficient staff.

If not working, I'll likely be cycling somewhere, on the yoga mat, paragliding, climbing or rowing.

What I Work With
